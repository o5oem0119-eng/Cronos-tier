# 🎨 [Chronos Tier] Image Prompt List: Episode 1

## 1. 전역 스타일 접미사 (Master Suffix)
모든 프롬프트 뒤에 아래 문구를 반드시 추가하십시오:
> `strictly lineless vector art, flat 2D animation style, Korean historical drama illustration, pure flat cel-shading with ZERO outlines, zero strokes anywhere, completely borderless, bold flat colors, rich color contrast, clean bright color blocking, solid color fills with sharp edges, colors separated by value and hue only, NO line art, NO black strokes, NO ink lines, NO sketch lines, NO visible brush strokes, NO texture, NO gradients, NO soft blending, NO light flares, NO 3D render, NO bokeh, NO glow.`

## 2. 장면별 상세 프롬프트 (Scene Prompts)


### [Scene A: Joseon Server Login]
- **Description:** A grand view of Gyeongbokgung Palace at dawn. The architecture is simplified into bold geometric shapes. High contrast between the dark purple mountains and the bright orange morning sky. No people, just the imposing silence of the palace.
- **Prompt:** `Grand panoramic view of Joseon Gyeongbokgung palace at dawn, bold geometric architectural shapes, deep purple mountains against bright orange sky, high contrast color blocking, cinematic composition, [Suffix]`

### [Scene B: Danjong's SSS Character Card]
- **Description:** A portrait of young King Danjong (12 years old) wearing a vibrant red 'Gonryongpo' (royal robe) with a golden dragon emblem. He has an innocent but elegant face. Behind him is a glowing golden aura. The composition resembles a high-end TCG card surface.
- **Prompt:** `Portrait of 12-year-old King Danjong in vibrant red royal Gonryongpo with gold dragon emblem, innocent and elegant face, golden aura backdrop, trading card game aesthetic, sharp cel-shading, [Suffix]`

### [Scene C: The Guardian Glitch (System Error)]
- **Description:** Four empty royal seats (thrones) lined up in a dark hall, illuminated by a flickering single candle. Large translucent red 'X' marks or 'DELETED' system text hovering over each seat. A sense of coldness and abandonment.
- **Prompt:** `Four empty Joseon royal thrones in a dark hall, single flickering candle light creating dramatic shadows, large translucent red system error UI overlays, cold and lonely atmosphere, sharp color contrast, [Suffix]`

### [Scene D: Suyang's Night Ambush]
- **Description:** Suyang-daegun standing on a traditional Korean stone bridge in deep blue moonlight. He is wearing a black 'Dallyeong' and his face is half-hidden by shadows, showing a sharp, ambitious eye. In the background, subordinates carry torches with vibrant orange flames (bold flat colors).
- **Prompt:** `Suyang-daegun on a stone bridge in deep blue moonlight, wearing black Joseon official robe, sharp ambitious eyes visible through shadows, soldiers with bright orange flat torches in background, dramatic low-angle lighting, [Suffix]`

## 3. UI 활용 가이드
- 이미지 생성 시 **화단 하단 30%**는 시스템 메시지 박스를 위해 단순하게 처리되었는지 확인하십시오.
- **스탯 바(Stats Bar)** 및 **HP 영역**은 리모션(Remotion) 코드에서 오버레이할 예정이므로 이미지 자체에는 포함하지 않습니다. (Prompt B의 배경 아우라만 유지)
